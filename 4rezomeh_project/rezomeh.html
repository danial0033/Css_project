Porsche

Porsche company records

Porsche is one of the most prominent car manufacturing companies in the world, founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931. Over the years, the company has endeavored to find a place in the global automotive market by designing and building high-quality cars. Initially, Porsche began as a consulting company in the field of car manufacturing and entered the automotive market in 1938 with the introduction of the Porsche 57 model. This unique and eye-catching car quickly caught the attention of luxury car enthusiasts. In the following years, Porsche strengthened its position in the global automotive market with the introduction of various models, including the Porsche 911, Porsche 924, and Porsche 944. Additionally, Porsche has always sought customer satisfaction by offering models with advanced technology and more features. In 2002, Porsche entered the SUV world with the introduction of the Cayenne model. This special and eye-catching car quickly gained popularity among SUV enthusiasts. Additionally, Porsche is pursuing a strong presence in the sustainable automotive market by offering electric models such as the Taycan. Given Porsche's long history and prominent position in the global automotive market, it is recognized as one of the top and most popular car manufacturing companies in the world. Additionally, Porsche seeks customer satisfaction by striving to offer high-quality cars and advanced technology.
